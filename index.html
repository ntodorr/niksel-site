Services

Three Phases.
One Reliable Partner.

We handle every cleaning phase of a construction project — from the first post-framing sweep through the final owner walkthrough. No handoffs. No excuses.

Phase 1 · Early Stage

Rough Clean

After mechanical, electrical & drywall — before flooring and finish work begins.

The foundation of a clean closeout. We clear the site of construction debris so your finish trades walk into a workspace — not a job site disaster.

  • Removal of drywall dust, debris, and scrap material
  • Sweeping and vacuuming all surfaces
  • Wiping down framing, windows, and rough fixtures
  • Clearing stairwells, corridors, and mechanical areas
  • Trash and debris removal
Phase 2 · Pre-Delivery

Final Clean

After all trades are done — before owner walkthrough or certificate of occupancy.

This is the clean that gets your project across the finish line. We go surface-by-surface to ensure the space is ready for the owner and ready to pass.

  • Deep clean of all interior surfaces — floors, walls, ceilings
  • Window and glass cleaning (interior)
  • Fixture, hardware, and appliance wipe-down
  • Cabinet, door, and millwork cleaning
  • Removal of stickers, labels, and protective film
  • Final floor polish or treatment as needed
Phase 3 · Punch & Closeout

Touch-Up / Punch Clean

After punchlist corrections — immediately before final inspection or owner delivery.

Targeted touch-ups after trades re-enter for punchlist work. We address specific areas quickly so you don't delay handoff over a dirty jobsite.

  • Spot cleaning of trade re-entry areas
  • Re-wiping of disturbed surfaces and fixtures
  • Touch-up floor cleaning and scuff removal
  • Final glass and mirror polish
  • Quick-turn turnaround to meet inspection timing
